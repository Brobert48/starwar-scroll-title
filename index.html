STAR WARSKOTOR RPG

Instructions

It is a period of war. And only you can decide the outcome.

Choose your Hero by clicking them.

Once you choose you Hero, your enemies will turn red.

Choose which enemy you will fight by clicking on them.

You may attack the with the 'Attack" button.

Combat Rules

Every time you attack, your Attack Power will increase by your base Attack amount.

You cannot heal at anypoint. So attack the weaker enemies first to build up your character's strength.
